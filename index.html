About Me

I am currently a Senior Research Scientist at SenseTime, ARFace group. If you are interested in applying for an internship or researcher/developer positions related to 3D face reconstruction, image stylization or avatar animation, please contact me at linchunze[at]sensetime.com.

I received my M.Eng. degree from Tsinghua University in 2019, advised by Professor Jiwen Lu and Professor Jie Zhou. I also received my M.Eng. degree (diplôme d'ingénieur) from Ecole Centrale de Nantes, France in 2019 as a dual degree program.

My research interests are computer vision, deep learning, image stylezation, 3D face reconstruction, pedestrian detection and face alignment.

Chunze Lin

I am currently a Researcher Scientist at SenseTime, ARFace group. If you are interested in intership or applied researcher/developer positions related to 3D face reconstruction, please contact me.

I received my M.Eng. degree from the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University in 2019, under the supervision of Professor Jiwen Lu. I also received my M.Eng. degree (diplôme d'ingénieur) from Ecole Centrale de Nantes, France in 2019 as a dual degree program. I got my B.S. degree in engineering from Ecole Centrale de Nantes, France in 2015.

My research interests are in computer vision, deep learning, pedestrian detection, 3D face reconstruction and face alignment.
